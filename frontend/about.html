About Royal Oak

Since 2010

A Journey of Flavour & Elegance

Royal Oak was founded by Chef Antonio Moretti with a singular vision: to create a dining destination that marries the rich traditions of global cuisine with contemporary sophistication.

What began as a small intimate bistro has grown into one of the city's most celebrated restaurants, earning accolades for our innovative menu, world-class wine selection, and unparalleled service.

Every dish at Royal Oak tells a story — from the sourcing of the finest seasonal ingredients to the artistic presentation on your plate. Our chefs bring experience from Michelin-starred kitchens across Europe, Asia, and the Americas.

Our Executive Chef

Executive Chef

Antonio Moretti

With over 20 years of culinary experience across three continents, Chef Antonio brings a world of flavour to Royal Oak. Trained at Le Cordon Bleu and having worked alongside Michelin-starred chefs in Paris, Tokyo, and New York, his cuisine is a celebration of global techniques and local ingredients.

"Cooking is an act of love. Every plate that leaves my kitchen is a reflection of my passion for bringing people together through exceptional food."
